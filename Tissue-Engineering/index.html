Regenerative Medicine

Biomineralization

Bioremineralization plays a crucial role in tissue engineering by promoting the formation of mineralized tissues like bone and teeth. Through the use of biocompatible materials and growth factors, bioremineralization can be utilized to enhance the regenerative capabilities of engineered tissues and promote their integration with the body.

##Bioprinting

3D printing enables the construction of complex tissue structures and organ-like systems in vitro, facilitating the development of personalized medicine and drug testing. By using this method, the creation of artificial blood vessels, heart valves, and even entire organs like the liver and kidney can be achieved.

Tough Hydrogel

Tough hydrogels have excellent mechanical properties. They are ideal materials for a wide range of applications in tissue engineering, drug delivery, and regenerative medicine. Durable, biocompatible implants, wound dressings, and drug delivery vehicles can be our future areas of exploration.
