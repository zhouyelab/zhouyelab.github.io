Drug Delivery

Smart Materials

A drug delivery system composed of smart materials can achieve the precise release of drugs in a specific physiological environment. In this way, we are developing smart materials that can be regulated by PH or temperature to accurately release therapeutic molecules such as hypoglycemic drugs and ROS whenever the peripheral environment changes rapidly, so as to control blood sugar or achieve anti-inflammatory purposes.
