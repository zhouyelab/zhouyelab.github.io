Dental Device

Implant Coating

Our team has conducted a series of studies on the properties of implant coatings, and conducted in-depth research on osteogenic, anti-inflammatory and antibacterial coatings.

Orthodontic Device

The surface coating of orthodontic devices can reduce the side effects of orthodontic process without affecting the mechanical properties of the materials. The coatings included membrane materials and traditional orthodontic materials.

AI Dentistry

AI Dentistry is the application of artificial intelligence in dentistry, which involves the use of computer algorithms and machine learning models to assist dentists in various dental procedures. This technology can help dentists in diagnosis, treatment planning, and predicting outcomes, as well as in detecting and analyzing oral diseases.
